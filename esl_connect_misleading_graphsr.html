B2/C1 Grammar: Describing Graphs, Trends and Cause/Result

The video explains how visual information can mislead, so this section asks students to describe graphs accurately using trend language, cautious interpretation and cause/result language.

Useful language: The graph appears to show... / There was a sharp rise in... / This may have been caused by... / As a result,... / However, the scale/context suggests...
