Work and Happiness

B2 lesson on success, income, meaning, earned success, service and joy at work.

Open video on YouTube

Can Work Make You Happy?

This B2 lesson helps students explore the difference between worldly success and real happiness at work.

Lesson aims

  1. Understand a video about happiness, work and success.
  2. Practise B2 vocabulary for careers, meaning, recognition and service.
  3. Complete a first-minute tapescript gap fill with 8 missing words.
  4. Use language for explaining causes, conditions and advice.
Lead-in:
  1. What makes a job satisfying: money, status, recognition, meaning, or helping others?
  2. Can someone be successful at work but still unhappy?
  3. What small things can make an ordinary job feel more meaningful?
  4. Would you prefer a high-paying job with little meaning or a lower-paid job that helps people?
  5. How important is recognition from managers or colleagues for happiness at work?

Grammar: Conditions and Purpose

Use B2 structures to explain what makes work meaningful and satisfying.

StructureUseExample
If + present, will / can + verbreal condition and resultIf people feel recognised, work can bring joy.
need to + verbnecessityYou need to seek earned success.
so that + clausepurposeManagers should recognise value so that people feel appreciated.
not only... but also...adding two important ideasWork should not only pay you but also serve others.
