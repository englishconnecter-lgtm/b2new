Can Creativity Be Learned?

This B2 lesson helps students discuss whether creativity is a natural talent or a skill that can be developed by creating the right conditions.

Lead-in:
  1. Do you think people are born creative, or can creativity be learned?
  2. When do you usually get your best ideas: when you are working, relaxing, talking to people, or doing something unrelated?
  3. Can doing something boring or unfamiliar make you more creative?
  4. What habits could help someone collect more ideas and experiences?

Grammar: Advice and Conditions

Use B2 structures to explain how people can create the right conditions for creativity.

StructureUseExample
can + verbability or possibilityYou can become more creative.
should + verbadviceYou should frame the problem clearly.
If + present, will / can + verbcondition and resultIf you collect experiences, you will have more ideas to connect.
by + -ingmethodYou can invite creativity by obeying your curiosity.
