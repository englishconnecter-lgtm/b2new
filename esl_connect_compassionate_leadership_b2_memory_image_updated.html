Compassionate Leadership: Giving Hard News

B2 lesson on leadership, empathy, compassion, difficult decisions and communication.

Open video on YouTube

Compassionate Leadership

This B2 lesson helps students understand the difference between empathy and compassion, especially when leaders have to give hard news.

Lead-in:
  1. What is the hardest kind of news a leader might have to give?
  2. What is the difference between understanding someone’s feelings and taking action to help them?
  3. Can delaying a difficult decision sometimes make the situation worse?
  4. What qualities does a leader need when communicating bad news?

Grammar: Obligation, Advice and Consequences

Use B2 structures to discuss leadership decisions and difficult communication.

StructureUseExample
have to + verbobligationSometimes leaders have to give hard news.
should + verbadviceLeaders should prepare before difficult conversations.
If + present, can / might + verbpossible consequenceIf leaders wait too long, it can become more damaging.
in order to + verbpurposeLeaders need courage in order to act compassionately.
